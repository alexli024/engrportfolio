Basic E-Nable Hand

This project was designed to learn e-Nable hub resources as well as familiarizing myself with 3d printed prosthetics. I had the pleasure of working with Chung for this project. We both arranged our own printable models in Ideamaker at 150%, mine is pictured above. We ended up using leftover parts from previous years to assemble our hand, which is why our model is multi-coloured. It worked out perfectly and I like the colour combination.
